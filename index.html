Clues

1.) The number of times I appear in my own representation is equal to my value. Explanation: Each digit in the number represents the frequency of that digit in the entire number. For example, if the number is 2020, there are two 0s, zero 1s, two 2s, and zero 3s.

2.) The digit in the first position idicates how many times zero occurs.

3.) The last digit of an autobiographical number represents the total count of digits in the number itself.

4.) For every subsequent position, a digit at position "N" indicates how many times digit "n" occurs.

5.) The sum of my digits is equal to my value.
